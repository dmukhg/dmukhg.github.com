Simple Interest, very simple September, 2011

On a very recent chai-coffee induced chat with Ram, I discovered the origin of the word 'interest' as used by banks and other lending entities.

Interest is the incentive you get for putting your money in a bank account or for lending your money out. It is your interest in that action. This may seem trivial but it seemed like a Eureka moment to me.
