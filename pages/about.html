About Aug, 2011

It is not very hard to describe yourself, especially if you have very few things to say. I am Dipanjan. I am a self-taught programmer. I love what I do. I am a geek, not a nerd. I spend most of my time in front of computer screen(s). When I am not, I like talking to people. The human experience and interfacing is as integral to me as programming.

I love abstractions. Python is what I use currently. I am slowly growing towards functional languages. Let's see how that works out.

About this site

This blog-cum-journal is intended to be a pensieve of sorts. Sorry for the HP allegory. I find, when I look at code that I have written a year back, I find stark differences both in my coding style and my thought processes. It is quite a revelation to observe yourself change. I started writing this blog so that I could look back some years from now and watch myself grow. Hopefully :).

This site is generated by sorry. It is a static-html generator that is powered by mako and markdown. It works for me, you could give it a shot too. If you like the results.
